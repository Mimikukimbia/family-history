Names in the Family

The following is information about family names and what they mean

My family's tribe

is called Baleyi. This meant tall people and most of my family were tall. However there were exceptions,

especially with my grandmother and father. The family name is Wafula, which means “born during the

rainy season”. Also there is a name in the family that is Nanyama. This means that it is the “time of

feasting”. Most of the family names meant according to the environment around them, like how my last

name means “born during the harvest season”.
