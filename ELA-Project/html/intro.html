Introduction to Family History

This is a brief summary of my family history

In my father's family he had 7 siblings and their ages ranged from 30-49 years old. Some of his siblings included

Alex Wafula, Issac Wafula and others. Their parents were Gabriel Onyako Wafula and Ruth Nanyama Wafula. Ruth

is currently alive at 66 years old and Gabriel died in 2018 at the age of 71. Ruth had 8 siblings and their ages ranged from

50-70 years old. The family's place of origin was Kenya and they had not migrated from any other country, town,

or city that we know of. No one served in the military or is indeginious and the family name is Wafula. The main orgin place

of the family of which we know of is Bungoma, Kenya and we still have family members living there to this very day.
