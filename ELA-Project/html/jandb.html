Life Growing Up

This is a rough summary of what life was like growing up for previous generations

In the past generations of my family we were mostly farmers. For my grandmother

her life was mostly to farm, herd the sheep, and go to school, but school did not have

a lot of emphasis. For my grandfather is was mainly the same. However my grandfather

had the choice to have education all the way to high school. Instead he only went up to 7th grade.

We do not know why he did that but gladly all of my father's brothers and sisters decided to go to high school

and finish it. I am here currently in the U.S because my father worked hard in school

and that got him accepted to a University in New York. From there he got a Phd and

decided to stay in the U.S.
