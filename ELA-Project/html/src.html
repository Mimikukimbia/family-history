Wafula, Ruth, Telephone Interview, 29 September 2024 Translation provided by Fred Wafula

The reason that I have little information about my family is because the census was useless where my

family came from. The idea of a census was to go to a house and check how many people lived there.

They did not ask for the names of the people, what their ages were, the religion of the family and ect. This

meant that I could not find any information about my past family on family search or find a grave.

The reason all sources are all interviews are because no information was found on the internet about my family members
